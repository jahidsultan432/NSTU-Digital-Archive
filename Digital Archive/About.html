The creation of a band of skilled manpower equipped with latest knowledge of science and technology is a must to achieve a prestigious position in this modern world. As a part of ensuring quality education, with immense expectations of the southern coastal inhabitants of Bangladesh, a new university named Noakhali Science and Technology University (abbreviated as NSTU) was established on 15 July 2001 enacting the Noakhali Science and Technology University Act 2001.Finally, it started its academic activities on 22 June 2006. The inclusion of the study of humanities implies the urge felt by the concerned authority to create leaders who will fashion a more humane and just world. The university not only engages itself in teaching and research but also provides societal services for the benefit of rural and urban populations.

Vision

To become the center of excellence for advanced research and technology to achieve sustainable development.

Mission

*To create precious manpower with intellectual, creative, technical and practical skills to contribute with moral, industry, community and caring relationship among all stakeholders.
*To provide outcome based standard education as preparation for advanced studies, entrepreneurship or future employment across a global range of ICT, business,
research organization along with social and public institution.
*To ensure the delivery of high-quality learning combining with hands-on training through qualified, dedicated and experienced facilitators.
*To deliver research and innovative opportunities at all levels to promote local and global networking for students and staff.
*To engage in partnerships with local and global communities, government agencies, industries and universities aspiring for sustainable society and economy.
