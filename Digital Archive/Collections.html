Generative AI in Libraries:Unlocking Creativity and Empowering Knowledge

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that is capable of creating new content or ideas without being explicitly programmed to do so.
It works by using algorithms to analyze existing data and then generating new content based on that analysis.
